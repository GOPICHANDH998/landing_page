The Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra 5G balances powerful hardware with advanced intelligence. Visually, it replaces sharp corners with subtly rounded edges, pairing a comfortable grip with a bright 6.8-inch display. Under the hood, the custom Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 processor and 16GB of RAM seamlessly run complex on-device AI tasks via Google Gemini Nano 2. For creators, the camera system features a 200 MP main sensor, a new 50 MP ultrawide lens, and dual telephoto zooms. Supporting 8K recording and 4K slow-motion at 120 fps, it delivers a highly versatile mobile workstation for modern productivity.
